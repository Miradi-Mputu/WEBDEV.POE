The Green Thumb Collective

About Us

Established in 2020 by siblings Maya and Liam, The Green Thumb Collective began as a passion project for sustainable gardening and quickly grew into a trusted local service. They started by offering basic garden maintenance to friends and family, and through word-of-mouth, expanded their services to include bespoke garden design, organic pest control, and native plant consultations. Their focus is on eco-friendly practices and creating beautiful, low-maintenance outdoor spaces.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission and Vision Statements: • Mission: To cultivate beautiful, sustainable, and thriving outdoor spaces for homes and small businesses in our community, fostering a deeper connection with nature through eco-conscious gardening practices. • Vision: To be the leading provider of sustainable landscaping solutions in our region, empowering individuals to create their green sanctuaries and contribute to local biodiversity.
